For educational and research purposes only. SmartValuation is a multi-method valuation tool built for learning fundamental analysis. It does not constitute financial advice, investment advice, or a recommendation to buy or sell any security.

All valuations are based on publicly available data from Screener.in and involve significant assumptions, approximations, and simplifications. Intrinsic value estimates are highly sensitive to input assumptions and should not be treated as precise or definitive.

Limitations: This model excludes qualitative factors (management quality, competitive moats, regulatory risk, ESG). DCF excludes working capital changes. Sector multiples from Damodaran are updated annually and may not reflect current market conditions. Composite weights are based on sector classification, not company-specific analysis.

Past cash flow growth does not guarantee future performance. Always conduct your own due diligence and consult a SEBI-registered investment advisor before making investment decisions.

Data freshness: Financial data is fetched live from Screener.in at time of analysis. Risk-Free Rate, ERP, and Damodaran multiples are updated manually.
